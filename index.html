About me

Creativity drives my life. I am pursuing the field of UX UI designing because I enjoy the whole journey of ideation, research, the technical process and the final outcome. The act of creating itself gives me a sense of satisfaction. I believe that there is always room for improvement and this motivates me to keep learning. This belief inspired me to do internships at Pinky Promise and Fluidesigns. To know more about my work experience, here is a link to my resume. You can also check my blog on medium which I had written for WorkDuck. I love other forms of creativity too. I enjoy sketching, drawing, writing quotes, dancing and reading books. I am currently studying engineering at IIT Kharagpur and will graduate in 2022. I am actively looking for a 2021 summer internship opportunity.
